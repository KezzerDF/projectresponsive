Inspiring online cooking classes

Seasoned cook or a complete beginner, we offer online cooking classes catered to help you learn, be inspired, gain knowledge, techniques and make cooking exciting.

Bring joy into your kitchen!

Book individual classes or join our cooking club. We run a cooking class each week. We cover a variety of cuisines, techniques, tips and tools. All classes are lead by our head chef Susan Jones and her team, who is a French celebrity chef and restaurateur. She has appeared on numerous reality television shows Head Chef, Peeled, Restuarants on the Up. Our classes are streamed online live, giving you the chance to ask questions, interact with other members and have fun learning and cooking together.

How does it work?

Book

Book an individual class or join our members cooking club to have access to all our classes. For cooking club members, sign in and reserve your spot through your account.

Prepare

Once booked on a class, you will receive an email with the link to access the online class and create your profile, along with the class recipes and list of ingredient you will need to purchase for the class.

Join

Use the link provided within your account to access the live class. All classes are recored and will be emailed after
